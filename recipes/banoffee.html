Banoffee Pie

Home

Banoffee pie is a British dessert made with banana slices, whipped cream, and caramel sauce. The original recipe, create in 1971, is attributed to the owner and chef of Hungry Monk Restaurant is Sussex. It's name is a combination of the words "banana" and "toffee"

Ingredients

  • Dulce de leche
  • Graham craker crumbs
  • Sugars
  • Ginger
  • Butter
  • Bananas
  • Heavy cream
  • Garnishes

How to make Banoffee Pie

  1. Make the crust, press it into the pie pan, and bake until set
  2. Arrange the dulce de leche and banana slices on top of the cooled crust
  3. Make the whipped cream and spread it over the bananas
  4. Chill the pie and garnish before serving
